## Scope

This audit inspected every durable `study-open`, its exact declared causal
question, changed and controlled variables, close, diagnosis, Batch, Job,
Executable registration, and scientific completion. It did not infer meaning
from display names, KPI rank, text similarity, or current source values.

The defect was systemic: Study identity bound the full protocol declaration,
but no durable question-core registry distinguished exact repeated estimands,
engineering reentry, continuation, confirmation, replication, and semantic
revision. This allowed a repaired successor to remain disconnected from the
gap it repaired and allowed exact repeated questions to look like unrelated
work. Conversely, a loose repair could have merged evidence that must remain
separate.

## Quant-Team Review

- Causality: exact changed and controlled variables decide whether a successor
  retains or changes the estimand; wording similarity alone decides nothing.
- Statistics and claims: a relation transfers no sample, multiplicity,
  uncertainty, scientific verdict, or confirmation authority.
- Data and sources: STU-0099 remains unresolved because no later eligible source
  establishes the missing point-in-time capability.
- Operations: engineering reentry requires an exact non-scientific gap and a
  successor completion; one failed Repair attempt is not scientific rejection.
- Architecture and portfolio: exact repeated questions remain connected for
  scheduling and reopen reasoning without collapsing their distinct Study,
  chassis, Executable, or Mission context.

The joint resolution accepts only the thirteen listed edges and three listed
distinct-core equivalences. Its claim boundary is question relation and

## Repaired Boundary

The repair adds one exact core from only causal question plus changed and
controlled variables, one unique binding per Study, and explicit typed lineage.
Natural-language similarity has no authority. A different-core non-revision
relation requires an exact accepted expert equivalence; semantic revision must
remain distinct and non-equivalent.

Question equivalence never means Study, Batch, Executable, trial, evidence,
claim, KPI, multiplicity, or negative-memory equivalence. A successor result
never rewrites its predecessor. Only an exact engineering reentry may record
that successor evidence resolves a predecessor non-scientific gap, and the
scientific evidence remains owned by the successor.

The complete history scan occurs only at the explicit stable-boundary backfill.
Prospective repeated-core checks use the fingerprint index and add core,
binding, equivalence, and lineage in the same Study-open event. Study close
adds only a successor-bound resolution record.
