## Purpose

This audit tests whether Project Axiom Rift is operating as an efficient,
truthful, forest-first quantitative research program. It covers historical
scientific decisions, source authority, implementation identity, validation
economics, software structure, state transitions, runtime boundaries, and
alignment with the persistent Project Goal. A finding is not closed by prose.
It requires a coherent repair, focused verification, and where necessary a
new typed state disposition or bounded evidence replay.

## P0 Findings

### A1. Discovery was judged by a universal confirmation-grade conjunction

Of 470 performance measurements, 462 were failed and 8 were not evaluable.
There were zero passes. All 462 non-null selection-aware p-values equal
1,000,000 ppm. The central discovery criteria require activity, economics,
stress, concentration, control superiority, global and paired uncertainty,
fold wins, and regime support to pass simultaneously. The same monthly
drawdown gate was applied outside the dense trend surfaces for which its own
claim limit was written.

Impact:

- partial positive ingredients were collapsed into whole-Executable failure;
- risk concentration was treated as proof that a signal ingredient was absent;
- unrelated historical questions increased the penalty on later discovery;
- all 103 scientific Batches declared candidate_eligible_on_pass false, so a
  pass could not directly justify a candidate anyway;
- candidate-grade conservatism consumed discovery throughput without adding a
valid candidate safety boundary.

Recomputing all 470 immutable plan/measurement pairs with component-aware
adjudication, without changing any old record, yields 422 partial_positive,
40 unresolved, and 8 not_evaluable states. This does not make 422 candidates
or winners. It proves that the old whole-Executable negative encoding erased
material claim structure and requires an additive qualification for every
legacy completion.

Required repair:

1. Separate validity, discovery component adjudication, confirmation, and
   candidate eligibility.
2. Treat causal, availability, cost, and finite-value failures as not evaluable.
3. Report effect, stress, stability, concentration, and uncertainty as distinct
   claim dimensions.
4. Apply a decision gate only when the preregistered profile makes that metric
   material to the causal question.
5. Keep discovery candidate-ineligible.
6. Move strong familywise and untouched-material authority to promoted
   confirmation, not every clue.

### A2. Project-wide hard-coded multiplicity is both overbroad and inaccurate

The discovery engine multiplies a conservative Monte Carlo p-value bound by a
cumulative project exposure count and takes the worst result over three block
lengths. Different Missions and causal questions are treated as one family.
Late Study modules also hard-code exposure counts that exceed immutable
Executable multiplicity. Examples include STU-0086 562 versus 553, STU-0092
585 versus 565, and STU-0104 595 versus 573.

Required repair:

- derive novel exposure from immutable Executable identity;
- never count reused controls as new exposures;
- bind raw p-value, concurrent family identity, family size, adjusted p-value,
  alpha allocation, and method;
- make unrelated-family additions invariant to an existing adjudication;
- retain the global search ledger as context, not a universal Bonferroni gate.

### A3. Operational Job failure and scientific verdict are conflated

There are 497 Job completions: 477 failed, 11 success, and 9 not evaluable.
Almost all failures are completed computations whose registered validator
returned a scientific falsification. This incorrectly exposes normal research
results to engineering retry and Repair semantics.

Required repair:

- a Job is operationally successful when all declared outputs were produced
  and validated;
- scientific passed, failed, and not_evaluable remain inside the validator
  result;
- Job failed is reserved for engineering, runtime, source, or external
  execution failure;
- negative memory is keyed to the scientific verdict, not Job status;
- old records remain valid legacy encodings and are not rewritten.

### A6. The USDJPY dependent lifecycle is fail-open

STU-0104 recorded 789 missing selected-high states. Missing source state retains

### A7. Study-close Git validation is a concrete research bottleneck

One successful full audit consumed 117.848 seconds, 545 Git subprocesses, and
2,060,815,283 stdout bytes. It issued 284 git-show calls and repeatedly parsed
the cumulative Journal snapshot for each of 83 prospective closes. The Writer
repeats this full-history audit at many routine scientific boundaries.

Required repair:

- strongly validate one new close snapshot at close time;
- commit a tamper-evident tracked checkpoint that binds its parent main commit,
  previous checkpoint, exact Journal boundary, close chain, repair manifest,
  control projection, and KPI projection;
- treat any ignored local cache only as a performance hint;
- on routine transitions authenticate the tracked checkpoint and validate only
  the bounded new suffix;
- keep a separate explicit full maintenance audit;
- use unique blob IDs and batch object reads during a rebuild;
- prove routine access cost is independent of historical close count.

### A8. Documented MT5 UTC and observed FPMarkets epoch coordinates conflict

The MetaTrader 5 Python reference documents UTC inputs and epoch timestamps.
On 2026-07-13, FPMarketsSC-Live package 5.0.5640 and terminal build 5833
returned mutually coherent tick/rate epochs about 10,800 seconds ahead of PC
UTC. The final sealed probe observed offsets 10,800, 10,800, and 10,799 seconds
for US30, US500, and USDJPY at 2026-07-13T14:34:18Z. Applying a UTC formatter
to those integers does not independently prove their absolute timezone.

Required repair:

- name the raw value MT5_epoch_coordinate;
- preserve both the documented UTC rule and exact runtime observation;
- infer neither broker timezone nor a fixed +3-hour or DST rule;
- apply no retroactive performance shift without evidence;
- leave absolute time, economic session, and historical point-in-time authority
  fail-closed until an independent mapping exists.

### A9. The first P0 replay design reused one Job identity for six subjects

The initial repair draft produced six validator artifacts with one Job id and
hash. StateWriter permits one exact scientific evidence subject per Job, so
those artifacts could not be closed canonically. The six axes were also chosen
after inspecting 470 historical completions. A within-six Bonferroni result
therefore cannot be called prospectively selection-corrected.

Required repair:

- create one new composite Executable, one v2 plan, and one measurement/result;
- retain the six immutable Executables only as ordered support evidence;
- label raw, max, Bonferroni, and Romano-Wolf results as within-replayed-set
  post-selection diagnostics;
- bind the complete historical-search context and grant no candidate,
  confirmation, or promotion authority;
- require untouched or separately permitted material for any later promotion.

### A10. Accepted Decision withdrawal was not a universal active-state rule

The additive withdrawal record correctly changed control direction, but legacy
readers could still scan the original Portfolio Decision and reuse it as a
chassis anchor, component-parity basis, Study permit basis, or terminal prune
evidence. An overlay that only some readers understand is not a safe state
transition.

Required repair:

- give every withdrawal an exact status stream;
- centralize active Decision resolution in StateWriter;
- make all execution, permit, parity, snapshot, and anchor consumers use the
  active resolver;
- remove Portfolio Decisions from scientific exhaustion evidence entirely.

### A11. Prospective v2 protocol enforcement was fail-open before activation

Scientific Job declaration enforced v2 only when a protocol head already
existed. After authority migration but before activation, a legacy registered
validator could therefore enter. Generic Jobs could also label an operational
failure `scientific_falsification` when no scientific binding was present.

Required repair:

- when the authority contract contains scientific_adjudication_v2, require an
  active protocol head bound to the current authority before any scientific
  Job declaration;
- reject scientific_falsification as a new operational failure kind for every
  Job, while retaining legacy read compatibility;
- preserve the rich claim/criterion adjudication in the completion record so
  later diagnosis and terminal logic cannot collapse it back to one verdict.

### A12. Rigid prune-and-quota exhaustion could hide omitted positive evidence

The original terminal path required every axis to be marked pruned and backed
by a whole-Executable negative memory. A first typed-disposition draft improved
the vocabulary but still allowed a caller to submit only a negative record and
omit a partial-positive completion from the same axis.

Required repair:

- use typed preserve, replay, reopen, defer, and retire-with-reason actions;
- derive effective state and candidate eligibility inside StateWriter;
- at terminal-only cost, enumerate every scientific completion on the exact
  Mission and axis, requiring its latest historical adjudication or rich
  prospective completion in the disposition;
- permit scientific exhaustion only for low-information retirement with exact
  durable negative evidence and the preregistered evidence depth;
- carry partial, invalid, not-evaluable, and unresolved axes forward with an
  explicit continuation or reopen condition.

### A13. Prospective multiplicity family registration was under-specified

A family_size value did not prove that the declared claim ids were the exact
ordered family or that every member produced a result. This allowed incomplete
families to look corrected.

Required repair:

- freeze ordered_member_ids and a family_registration_hash in the plan;
- bind every criterion and result to one exact member_id;
- require registered count, family_size, and measurement completeness to agree;
- retain point and Monte Carlo upper values separately.

### A14. Historical correction was initially caller-controlled and non-monotone

An additive legacy overlay is itself a scientific authority boundary. An early
repair draft allowed its caller to provide a multiplicity and risk profile,
allowed a later overlay to omit a prior source invalidation, and did not
categorically exclude a rich v2 completion from historical re-adjudication.
That could manufacture a more favorable legacy interpretation or erase an
invalidity without changing the immutable trial.

Required repair:

- accept historical overlays only for the fixed legacy-v1 result shape;
- derive the compatibility profile inside StateWriter rather than from the
  caller;
- derive source-authority latches from durable state and carry their union
  monotonically across every later overlay;
- reject any attempt to re-adjudicate a rich v2 completion;
- preserve zero trial, candidate, holdout, and negative-memory mutation.

### A15. Withdrawal and source-audit evidence were initially provenance-lax

The first generic withdrawal route could target an accepted Decision using
free-form rationale without binding the exact report finding, accepted
Decision, snapshot, target axis, baseline, source head, and reason. It could
also lose scheduler constraints when direction was restored. Source audit facts
spread across unrelated report paragraphs could be assembled as if they were
one finding, and an ordinary suspended source state could avoid receiving the
later audit latch.

Required repair:

- require a typed withdrawal manifest over one exact accepted-but-unstarted
  basis and one immutable report artifact;
- require all source correction facts in one exact ASCII finding block;
- restore the scheduler constraints that existed before the withdrawn
  Decision;
- allow the durable audit latch to attach after an ordinary suspension and
  then make it union-monotone;
- reject forged, scattered, stale-head, or already-started withdrawal bases.

### A16. Mission terminal logic could claim exhaustion with no exhausted axis

A typed disposition vocabulary alone did not prevent every axis from being
carried forward while the Mission still closed as exhausted. Candidate-positive
evidence could also be described as resolved without binding the actual
candidate and disposition streams. Some terminal entry points did not all pay
the Study-close delivery guard, and the contracts described an in-Mission
exhaustion-standard amendment route that had no single-writer implementation.

Required repair:

- require at least one genuinely low-information, scientifically exhausted
  axis before an exhaustion terminal is accepted;
- derive candidate resolution from the actual candidate and latest typed
  disposition streams;
- invoke the same Study-close guard from exhaustion acceptance and Mission
  close;
- keep numeric floors and required evidence modes immutable inside a Mission;
- route an audited standard defect to the successor Mission rather than an
  undocumented authority bypass.

### A17. An ignored local delivery cache was forgeable authority

The first performance repair treated an ignored local high-water as sufficient
to skip historical verification. A copied or forged cache could therefore hide
an uncheckpointed Study close, while another checkout could not reconstruct the
same trusted boundary. Performance state cannot silently become delivery
authority.

Required repair:

- make the authenticated high-water a tracked canonical JSON checkpoint;
- bind its digest in the commit message and bind every successor to the prior
  checkpoint commit and digest;
- require the checkpoint, control, Journal, and KPI paths to be staged
  atomically for a real Study close;
- reject worktree tampering, checkpoint omission, history rewrite, and a forged
  ignored cache;
- permit full-audit initialization only after the complete first audit passes.

### A18. The repaired forest was blocked by rigid synthesis composition

The original Portfolio rules rejected every pure SYNTHESIS axis, even when the
question was exactly whether an already registered forest should be evaluated
as one composite instead of six cherry-picked subjects. Reconstruction of the
current 32 axes also depended on a repeated ignored local helper rather than a
strict reusable projection from durable manifests.

Required repair:

- permit only the narrow pure-SYNTHESIS case with a typed architecture chassis,
  every architecture role populated, and the complete decision-domain control
  set;
- keep a lone PORTFOLIO axis and incomplete synthesis compositions rejected;
- rehydrate Executable, chassis, and PortfolioAxis objects from durable
  component manifests with exact identity checks;
- verify all 32 current axes round-trip exactly before opening the audit Study.

## P1 Findings

### B1. Mission exhaustion was accepted at exact minimum counts

MIS-0001 closed at exactly 7 axes times 2 negative Executables. MIS-0002 through
MIS-0005 each closed at exactly 5 axes times 2. The 64 involved legacy Studies
do not have the current typed research-layer and architecture classifications.
The terminal records remain historically valid transitions, but their
Project-Goal-level exhaustion credit must be qualified. Minimum quota is not
evidence that useful partial positives, invalid measurements, and alternative
architectures are saturated.

### B2. Negative memory loses claim dimensions

All 462 memories are whole-Executable falsifications. STU-0038 and STU-0039
record broad evidence-mode credit despite zero trades. STU-0061 produced
positive native and stress economics with five winning folds and two regimes,
but the memory cannot express that economics survived while concentration and
uncertainty did not.

Required repair:

- preserve immutable negative memory;
- add claim-level qualification with supported, contradicted, unresolved, and
  invalid states;
- award evidence modes only to evaluable claims;
- prevent an exact surface prune from becoming a mechanism-family ban.

### B3. Repair history sometimes created a new scientific Study

The following chains should be interpreted as one causal question plus an
engineering correction, not independent scientific failures:

- STU-0053 to STU-0054;
- STU-0070 to STU-0071;
- STU-0073 to STU-0083;
- STU-0097 to STU-0098;
- STU-0103 to STU-0104.

STU-0018 to STU-0019 repeated historical source validation for a metadata TTL
addition six minutes later. STU-0094 is a genuinely later runtime recertification
and is not the same case. STU-0045 has four not-evaluable Jobs but a
not-supported Study close and requires an additive outcome qualification.

### B4. The nominal forest is scheduled mostly as a serial branch

Of 193 Portfolio Decisions, 156 compare two options and 35 compare three. Only
two compare four or five. In the latest 100 Decisions, 93 have two options.
The registry is broad, but scheduling mostly adds or removes one branch at a
time. A material Decision should compare the live frontier across information
value, identifiability, expected cost, opportunity cost, and architecture,
without forcing ceremony for routine repair.

### B5. Architecture family identity is too implementation-sensitive

Forty typed Studies produced 30 architecture families and only one architecture
review. Implementation-level surface hashes can split semantically equivalent
chassis families. Semantic roles and boundary bindings should define the stable
family. Artifact identity belongs in Component and Executable identity, with
equivalence evidence when appropriate.

### B6. Source freshness blocks offline historical research

Six-hour eligibility receipt age is required even for historical Batches. This
caused dedicated runtime refresh and recertification work before offline
development evidence. Historical content/semantics authority and runtime
freshness must be separate capabilities.

### B7. The codebase grows by temporal Study file

Current tracked src contains about 91,723 Python LOC. Research contains about
73,083 LOC in 199 files. Ignored local Python adds about 39,489 LOC. Conservative
AST analysis found at least 5,565 exact duplicate function lines. StateWriter is
about 13,592 LOC, while Study-specific runners and validators duplicate core
fold, evidence, and result construction.

Required direction:

- keep StateWriter as the only write-capability facade;
- extract pure transition policy and transaction primitives behind it;
- use reusable component primitives, a generic evaluation engine, declarative
  StudySpec, and a common scientific result bundle;
- freeze historical modules and identities until explicit parity permits safe
  replacement;
- add tests for reusable semantics, not every new Study composition.

### B8. Indexed storage still performs history-proportional reads

Routine paths use unbounded records_by_kind queries. Authority validation for an
old row can reread and hash a 31.96 MB sealed segment. A single sealed-row check
measured about 39.1 ms versus 0.91 ms for the active segment. Sealed segment
verification needs a process cache and row access needs offset seek or batched
segment validation. Routine transitions need keyed views.

### B9. The default test command can succeed with zero tests

python -m unittest discovers zero tests and exits successfully. Pytest collects
576 tests, but pytest is not declared in the test extra and testpaths are not
configured. Pytest must be the documented canonical runner. Full-suite execution
must remain operator-selected; focused surface checks are the normal path.

### B10. Two identity regression fixtures lagged the repaired source semantics

The complete repaired suite initially exposed two US30 tests whose expected
architecture and Executable identities still described the pre-repair source
implementation. The production identity correctly changed because the source
contract and implementation bytes changed. The fixtures were updated to the
new derived identities while preserving the separately registered legacy hash
used for historical reconstruction. This is verification debt, not parity
evidence for reusing the old identity.

## Findings Not Supported By The Audit

- No 2/3-hour numeric historical performance shift was demonstrated. A
  quarantine-safe US100 development frame and its stored MT5 coordinate history
  matched 560,552 rows; 1,947 samples across every month matched at zero relative
  offset. The live runtime probe nevertheless contradicts an unqualified UTC
  interpretation. Existing coordinate-aligned PnL is not rewritten, while
  absolute-time and session claims remain unavailable.
- No bfill, centered rolling, same-day future rank, global scaler, or full-OOS
  preprocessing leakage was found in the inspected paths.
- Completed-bar decision to next exact bar-open execution was preserved in the
  common simulator.
- Fold-trained selectors and models were isolated inside train boundaries in
  the inspected implementations.
- The listed engineering failures were generally repaired. The dominant defect
  is misclassification and duplicate scientific bookkeeping, not a universal
  one-attempt abandonment policy.
- VIX source pruning remains appropriate because roll, constituent, and
  adjustment semantics are not identifiable from the available source.

## First Repair Implementation And Verification

The reusable repair surface is implemented before canonical activation:

- scientific_adjudication_v2 separates validity, component evidence,
  multiplicity, diagnostic risk, decisive risk, confirmation, and candidate
  authority, and preserves its rich adjudication in the Job completion;
- additive historical adjudication qualifies all 470 legacy completions without
  rewriting a trial, close, or negative memory, uses a Writer-derived fixed
  legacy profile, rejects v2 re-adjudication, and preserves the union of durable
  source invalidations;
- source audit invalidation binds a canonical per-source manifest to the exact
  audited source-state head and permanently latches that SourceContract;
- accepted-but-unstarted Decision withdrawal binds one exact report finding and
  accepted basis, restores prior scheduler constraints, has one status stream,
  and uses a common active resolver for every execution consumer;
- axis dispositions preserve forest state and require the complete scientific
  evidence set for that Mission and axis, actual candidate-disposition streams,
  and at least one genuinely exhausted axis for an exhaustion terminal;
- the P0 replay uses one composite Executable and treats its six selected
  historical children as post-selection support only;
- strict durable Portfolio projection rehydrated all 32 current axes to their
  exact identities without an ignored Study-local helper;
- prospective multiplicity families bind exact ordered members and complete
